Coding4designers

The central website for the coding4designers as part of the course on Emerging Technologies 2023.

The aim of this course, is to provide a state of the art overview of current and emerging technologies in light of the 4th industrial revolution. The goal is to inform and inspire future industrial designers by providing an overview of emerging technologies and the opportunities they offer for future society. This technology is characterized by the ever-thinning boundary between physical, digital and biological spheres. How is this (by now ubiquitous) technology embedded in products, society and even the human body?

The potential of billions of people connected to mobile devices, with unprecedented computing power, storage capacity and access to knowledge is unprecedented. All these possibilities are provided by technological developments in the field of artificial intelligence, robotics, Internet of Things, web technology, sensor technology, nanotechnology,...
